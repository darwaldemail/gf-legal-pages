Admin backend FAQ

  • “Sign in” doesn’t work — Ensure your preview/host domain is in Authentication → Settings → Authorized domains. Google pop-ups may be blocked; allow pop-ups and try again.
  • Image upload fails — Confirm you’re signed in and your email is allowed by Storage Rules for the path /images/**. Also verify the bucket is food-app-backend-c724a.firebasestorage.app in your Firebase config.
  • Preview shows “Meal photo preview” text — The editor now prefers public download URLs after upload. Re-pick the image so it saves imageUrl for that item, then Save.
  • Publish to Firebase (Firestore) — Make sure Firestore Rules allow public read on /appData/menu and authenticated write for admins.
  • Legal pages hosting — Host these pages on a separate Firebase Hosting site to avoid overwriting your backend UI.
